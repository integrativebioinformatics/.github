# Laboratory of Integrative Bioinformatics (LIB)

**_Universidad de Chile_**

> _Faculdad de Ciéncias Químicas y Farmacéuticas (FaCiQyF)_
> 
> _Unidad de Genómica Avanzada (UGA)_

The Laboratory of Integrative Bioinformatics, led by **Dr. Vinicius Maracaja-Coutinho**, focuses on the development and application of computational methods to study complex biological systems.

### Research Areas

  * **Transcriptional & Genomic Complexity:** Investigating the intricate architecture of genomes and the dynamic nature of the transcriptome across different biological contexts.
  * **Systems Biology:** Multi-omics integration and gene co-expression network analysis.
  * **Bioinformatics Tools:** Development of open-source software for genomic and transcriptomic data processing.
  * **Precision Medicine:** Genomic studies focused on Latin American populations.
